STAR Mission

The mission of the Supporting Transformative Autism Research (STAR) project is to improve the lives of individuals with autism and their families through ground-breaking research and innovative models for care, support, and education. It was designed to ignite discovery, improve systems of care, and equip communities with best practices to support people with autism across the lifespan. STAR brings together researchers, community partners, individuals with autism and their families to identify challenges and co-develop innovative solutions.

AUTISM DRIVE

The AUTISM DRIVE (Data System for Research Integration, Visualization & Exchange) was developed as a dynamic system for secure collection and sharing of knowledge and information. The DRIVE helps researchers collaborate with the autism community to gain insights into autism and to develop meaningful solutions to challenges experienced by individuals with autism and their families. It also provides individualized online access to training and resources for individuals with autism, their families, and the professionals who support them.

About the Autism Registry

The purpose of the Autism Registry is to connect individuals with autism and their families with research opportunities and resources in their own communities. By joining the Registry, we can share information with you that is tailored to your needs and interests. We can also match you with research opportunities that might be a good fit for you and your family. Your participation will help us learn more about autism so we can create new solutions to achieve positive outcomes. Register now!
