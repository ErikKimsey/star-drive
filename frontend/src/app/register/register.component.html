Why should I join?

By joining Autism DRIVE, we will be able to let you know about resources and upcoming events in your community. We can also match you with research opportunities that might be a good fit for you and your family. Your participation will help us learn more about autism so we can create new solutions to achieve positive outcomes.

What does it involve?

We will ask you to answer some questions about yourself or your family member with autism. We will use this information to provide you with the most relevant information about resources and upcoming studies. These questions should take about 10-15 minutes. If you don’t finish all the questions at once, you can save your progress and return later. You can also come back and modify your information or cancel your account at any time.

Your Privacy

Only UVA autism researchers will be able to see the information you submit. We will only use your information to connect you with potential research opportunities and relevant resources. Because of this, researchers may contact you through the Autism DRIVE about research opportunities that may be relevant or interesting to you.

Information you share through the registration process will be stored on a secured server. To keep your information anonymous, your identifying information and any sensitive healthcare information will be separated and stored on another secure server behind a firewall. The registration process will notify you of what is sensitive as you go through the registration. The data security of the servers we are using have been reviewed and approved by the University of Virginia’s Information Technology Services. Our registration process meets the University of Virginia Institutional Review Board’s ethical guidelines.

If you want to cancel your account at any time, you can let us know by emailing uvastar@virginia.edu.
